Muffin Recipe

A basic recipe. You can add pretty such anything to it!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 200g caster sugar
  • 250ml milk
  • 125ml vagetable oil
  • 400g plain floor
  • 3tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Method

  1. Preheat oven to Gas Mark 6 or a medium temperature. line muffin tray with paper muffin case.
  2. Beat the eggs in a mixing bowl and then mix in the oil and milk. Add the sugar and whisk until dissolved.
  3. Sift in the dry ingredients: flour, baking powdr and salt and mix until smooth. NB: and any extra ingredients now eg. chocolate chips/fruit.
  4. Fill muffin case TWO THIRDS full (this is important for the perfect size muffins) and bake in the middle of the oven for 25 minutes (approx. depending on your oven).
  5. Leave to cool (or enjoy warm!).
